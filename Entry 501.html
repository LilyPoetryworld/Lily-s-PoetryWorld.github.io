Entry 501

As the sun goes down, a cloud of somber gray invades

And from the blue of the sky, the orange of joy fades

I crave an awaited sunrise of surprise,
Bit by bit,my languid heart is obliged to rise
Only to fall into a sleep of apathy again
Where nightmares of disappointment fuel the perpetual pain;
I sit by the rocks,on our old spot,wondering "Where did my baby go?"
Perhaps my unjust winds of expectations extinguished our love's fiery glow
Why did he leave me wretched,knowing how I had no control
Perhaps,the tight grasp of my palm shattered his delicate soul.
O' Goddess of Love,
I burn fiercely with the desire to repent
In all earnest,I seek and deserve punishment
I wish to see my veins writhing in pain
Torment me for breaking what can't be created again,
But until you do so
My fingers shall recklessly play
With the shattered glasses of love each day,
And let them pierce right through my skin.
Rendering them drenched in crimson tears of an unredeemed sin.

Lily
