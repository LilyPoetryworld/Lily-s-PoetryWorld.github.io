Nature's Carol

Embracing the enticing brightness of the day,

mysterious mother nature seems surprisingly gay,
in the sweet company of the morning sun and its gracious light,
half-covered,half-uncovered by clouds of fading and forming white

Chipping of birds from the sky overhead,
provoke the restless fauna to tread,
uncovered distances in search of prey,
driving the drowsy silence of the earth away;

With inpatient and longing glances,
the buzzing bees look for spring in the swaying branches,
of tall , protruding mango trees,
whose sweet smell seduces the summer breeze;
The resplendent streaks of fruits against rich velvety green,
embellish the serenity of the ornate scene.

In yet another segment of mother nature's lullaby,
under the silhouette of the warm blue sky,
the crickets squeak, the nightingale sings
the kookaburra giggles hard while the sparrow flaps its wings;
With the reckless flora in the land of the wild,
the grasshopper plays peek and hide, like a mischievous child;
Craggy woods, lush green fields, and the gentle hills,
play host to
Wide-eyed sunflowers, playful daisies, and the dancing daffodils

Vibrant butterflies adorn the freshness of the air,
halting, for a moment the chaos and despair .
Gloomy earth rejoices , and celebrations spread,
awakening the autumnal spirit of the fading dead

Secrets embedded in the white blossom of the moon,
whispers tales foretelling the inevitable doom
of each fallen star's everlasting shine,
and mother nature's wondrous whine
as she melts into the night's tranquil face,
swallowed by darkness's fleeting grace.

Lily
