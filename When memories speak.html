When memories speak

A memory embedded deep in my heart,
Whispers your name each night and tears me apart,
I close my eyes, longing to fall asleep;
Slowly into another dream of falsehood I creep
Where you take me back to the day we met,
To a time when love had not blossomed yet

I stare at the ghosts of our past,
to cherish the moments that destiny had cast.
Looking for the redness that graced my cheeks when you held my hands,
I reminisce how you caressed my hair's messy strands;
Oh how lovingly a bright smile
graced your cherry lips,
As I planted soft kisses on your gentle fingertips

Your hands around my waist, my hands around your neck,
were met by a soft breeze that twirled
near our melted flesh.
Before fading away with a wistful sigh,
It looked right through your hollow eyes
Searching for a love that never was

I always knew how it would end
But for a few moments of peace with you I chose to play pretend
Eventually I became the slave of a knife
That would stab me for the rest of my life

Lily
