In His Embrace

On this sultry afternoon, I dream of wallowing in the comfort of your embrace;

And rest my head on the softness of your chest;

Oh how it heaves rhythmically while uttering my "name";

I press my ears against it to hear the enticing thumps and beats;

Tilting my head a little, I look above, to cast a look of awe on your contented face,
as it smiles back at me;
Your arms then slowly behold my chest from behind and draw me closer;
I rest my nose in the crook of your neck to take in the sweet fragrance of "Home";
Symphonies of a lullaby escape your lips;
You sing softly, then swiftly, then softly again;
Melodies immerse into my ears,
just as the ocean waves hit the shore, and retreat back as if bring more music in their crannies;
My hand searches recklessly for your hand to interlock our fingers,
And fasten the strings of our soul with a tight knot, praying that they never depart;
While tenderly caressing my thumb with yours, you say, "I am here, Aalu";
"I am here"
The other hand reaches over my eyes, and covers them with the silhouette of unsaid promises and serene love;
I shut my eyes,
The light of consciousness goes dim
All I see is the sight of your kind eyes before
A sleep like never before invades

Lily
